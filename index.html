Pasta

al Limone

  • Servings: 2

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes

  • Cook Time: 20 minutes

  • Total Time: 30 minutes

This quick and delicious pasta dish is the perfect way to use up a summer bounty of basil and Lemons!

Don't have a green thumb? Never fear! You can knock out this classic pasta dish in no time with some Lemons and Parmisan Cheese.

The Ingredients

  • 8 oz. spaghetti or linguine pasta
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, plus 2-4 reserved leaves for garnish
  • 2 knobs of butter
  • 1 fresh lemon
  • 1 tsp of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus 2 tbsp. for garnish.
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • pinch of salt

Variation: Got no time, or no fresh lemons? Use a storebought lemonjuice instead.

The Process

  1. Bring 4 quarts of water to a rolling boil in a large pot. Salt the water generously and add the pasta.
  2. While the pasta cooks squeeze your lemons and cook the squeezed lemons in a separate pot of water.
    • Remove stems from basil leaves.
    • Grate your Parmesan cheese
  3. Once the pasta is halfway done remove about 2/3 of the water
  4. turn the heat down to medium low
  5. Add in your lemon juice plus some of the lemon water on the side until it covers the pasta
  6. let it cook for anothe minute and then add your butter while stirring
  7. Once the butter has melted add in your cheese and kill the heat
  8. As soon as your sauce is nice and creamy the pasta is ready to serve
  9. Garnish with reserved basil leaves and Parmesean and serve. Buon Appetito!
